Miura SV

This project showcases my ability to blend classic automotive beauty with modern AI-driven video techniques. By leveraging AI tools for color grading and motion enhancement, I brought a vintage icon to life with a fresh, cinematic perspective. The result is a seamless fusion of nostalgia and innovation, demonstrating my commitment to pushing creative boundaries in visual storytelling.

Luxury Humanoid

In this experimental piece, I explored the intersection of luxury design and artificial intelligence. Using AI-assisted animation and compositing, I crafted a futuristic humanoid character that embodies both elegance and technological advancement. This project highlights my skills in character design, 3D integration, and the creative use of AI to elevate visual narratives.

Curious Tiger

Curious Tiger is a testament to the power of AI in wildlife storytelling. By utilizing AI-based video enhancement and frame interpolation, I was able to capture and emphasize the subtle emotions and movements of the subject. This work demonstrates my ability to use cutting-edge technology to create engaging, emotionally resonant content that stands out in today's digital landscape.

Gravity Tracksuit

This video experiment leverages AI-driven motion tracking and effects to visualize the concept of gravity-defying apparel. The project involved advanced compositing and the creative application of AI tools to simulate dynamic, physics-based interactions. It reflects my passion for merging technology with fashion and my ability to deliver visually compelling concepts for innovative brands.

Pigeon finds gum

In this event video, I used AI-powered editing tools to streamline the post-production process, enhancing both pacing and visual clarity. The playful narrative is brought to life with subtle AI-driven effects that maintain a natural, authentic feel. This piece demonstrates my efficiency in workflow and my knack for infusing everyday moments with creative flair.

War of Worlds

For this animation, I combined traditional VFX techniques with AI-enabled video upscaling and scene reconstruction. The result is a high-impact, cinematic sequence that pushes the limits of what's possible in independent production. This project underscores my expertise in integrating AI into the creative pipeline to achieve results that rival big-budget studios.
